Kiribath(Milk Rice)


Ingredient

  • 500 grams rice

  • white 2 cups water

  • 2 cups coconut milk thick

  • salt to taste


Method

Step 01

Wash the rice, put in a medium saucepan and add water.

Step 02

Bring to the boil in medium heat, cover the saucepan,
cooking until the rice is soft, 15-20 minutes.

Step 03

Add coconut milk and salt, stir well, and reduce heat to low,
continue cooking in low heat for another 10 minutes,
until the milk has been absorbed.

Step 04

coconut milk is absorbed, and the rice is very soft and creamy.
By now rice milk is ready.

Step 05

Transfer the rice milk to a flat plate and fatten
down with piece of butter paper.

Step 06

If you like any shape, you can use a cookie cutter,
serve with fish, chicken curry, banana.
