Kokis

Ingredient

  • White raw rice flour - 500g

  • Sugar - 2 teaspoon

  • turmeric powder - 1 teaspoon

  • egg - 1

  • coconut milk - 1 cup

  • Salt - 2 teaspoon

  • Oil -As required


Method

Step 01

1 in a bowl take flour, turmeric powder, salt, sugar, salt, egg and gradually
add Â½ cup of coconut milk in order to form a paste.

Step 02

2 mix well and keep it aside for 2 hours.

Step 03

3 take the batter and add the rest of the coconut milk and mix it well.

Step 04

1 heat some oil in a pan and dip your mould in it.

Step 05

2 dip the hot mould into the batter and again into the hot oil until it turns golden brown.

Step 06

3 detach the kokis from the mould using a knife and cook it from both the sides.
