Kiribath(Milk Rice)


Kiribath (Milk Rice) is a traditional Sri Lankan rice dish prepared by adding coconut milk when cooking the rice.
Kiribath is a renowned traditional Sri Lankan dish, which is prepared and eaten during auspicious moments.

Kokis


Kokis is a deep-fried, crispy Sri Lankan food made from rice flour and coconut milk.
Although considered as a traditional Sri Lankan dish, it is believed to have come from the Dutch.
This is an important dish when celebrating Sinhala New Year and plays a major role in the festivities.
